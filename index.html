About the Course

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) teaches how to design efficient algorithms and evaluate performance. It builds analytical thinking that helps solve computational problems and forms the base for advanced computer science concepts.

Project Overview

The "City Design" project applies DAA principles to model and optimize city planning. Using algorithmic logic, it simulates traffic management and resource optimization for smarter, efficient urban infrastructure.

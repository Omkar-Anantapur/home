About the Course

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) is at the core of computer science. It enables us to solve problems efficiently and helps in understanding the logic behind computational thinking. This project applies algorithmic principles to urban design — showcasing how computational ideas can help model and optimize aspects of a modern city.

Project Overview

The “City Design” project integrates algorithmic approaches in urban planning to simulate traffic management, optimize road networks, and visualize city layouts. By applying data structures and algorithms, we aim to bring efficiency and sustainability to digital city models.
